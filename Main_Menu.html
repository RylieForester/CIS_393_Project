WELCOME TO MY BOOK RECOMMENDATION WEBSITE!

Here, you are able to select from a wide variety of book genres, including fiction, nonfiction, and poetry, and receive book recommendations based on your chosen preferences.

This website also features a wishlist option, wherein a user is able to save book titles that appeal to them to an individual wishlist.

To get started, click either the 'RECOMMENDATIONS' button to receive recommendations, or the 'WISHLIST' button to view a saved wishlist. Navigation buttons can be found at the top of the website.
